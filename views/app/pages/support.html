Frequently Asked Questions

How do I complete KYC verification?
To complete KYC verification, go to your profile and select "KYC Verification". You'll need to upload a government-issued ID and take a selfie. The process typically takes 24-48 hours for approval.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept bank transfers, PayPal, credit/debit cards, and various local payment methods. The available options depend on your location. Check the payment methods section when posting or responding to ads.
How long does a trade take to complete?
Most trades are completed within 15-30 minutes. The time depends on payment method, bank processing times, and how quickly both parties respond. Escrow protection ensures your funds are safe during the process.
What are the trading fees?
Trading fees are 0.5% for takers and 0.3% for makers. There are no fees for deposits or withdrawals. Premium members get reduced fees and additional benefits.
How do I dispute a trade?
If you encounter issues during a trade, click the "Dispute" button in the chat. Our support team will review the case within 24 hours. Provide screenshots and evidence to help resolve the dispute quickly.
